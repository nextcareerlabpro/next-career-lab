Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data

Last Updated: 1 May 2026

1. Introduction

Upgrade Your Resume ("we", "us", or "our") is operated by Ashish Kumar Sharma, Shahjahanpur, Uttar Pradesh, India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

a) Account Information:

  • Name and email address (via Google Sign-In)
  • Profile photo (from Google account)

b) Usage Data:

  • Resume text you upload or paste
  • Job descriptions you submit for analysis
  • AI tool usage history
  • Pages visited, features used (via Google Analytics GA4)

c) Payment Data:

  • Transaction ID and payment status (via Razorpay)
  • We do NOT store your card or bank details — all payment processing is handled by Razorpay

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve our AI-powered career tools
  • To manage your account and Pro subscription
  • To send payment confirmation and Pro activation emails
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the platform
  • To prevent fraud and ensure platform security

4. Data Storage

Your account data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore). Resume content submitted for analysis is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored on our servers. Google Analytics data is stored on Google's servers as per their privacy policy.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication & database
  • Google Analytics (GA4) — Usage analytics
  • Razorpay — Payment processing
  • Groq AI — AI analysis engine
  • Vercel — Hosting & deployment

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We are not responsible for their data practices.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. Data is only shared with the third-party services listed above as necessary to operate the platform.

7. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for authentication (Firebase) and analytics (Google Analytics). By using our Service, you consent to the use of these cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect platform functionality.

8. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of analytics tracking

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@upgradeyourresume.com

9. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, Firebase security rules, and secure payment processing via Razorpay. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

10. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via email or a notice on the platform.

12. Contact

For privacy-related queries, contact:
Email: support@upgradeyourresume.com
